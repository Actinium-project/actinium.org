About

Our initial goal was not only to develop yet another cryptocurrency, but to provide a complete environment for mining, faucets, explorers, and a mobile wallet. The next big step we want to achieve is to bring our coin onto the Lightning Network.

Being technologically compatible to and as scarce as Litecoin, we have all the needed features already activated to achieve this goal. The possibilities of 2nd-Layer solutions are numerous and we don't want to remain a blockchain-only cryptocurrency. The inevitable progress will deliver competing solutions to many problems. On different layers.
